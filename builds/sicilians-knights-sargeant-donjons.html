🛡️
The Sicilians are one of the toughest civilisations in the game. Your units take less damage than normal. You have a special tower called the Donjon. You have powerful unique soldiers called Serjeants. This guide will tell you exactly what to do and when.
Your special bonuses — remember these
🪨 You start with +100 stone already
🏰 You can build a Donjon right away — it's a powerful tower
🛡️ Your soldiers take 40% less bonus damage — they are tougher than they look
🌾 Your farms give much more food than other civs
⚡ Your walls and towers build 50% faster
⚔️ Hauberk — makes your knights much harder to kill (research in Castle Age)
👑 First Crusade — spawns free Serjeants at every Town Center (save for Imperial Age)

Castle Age — Which army should I build?
Look at what your opponent is doing. Then choose below.
🏇 Path A — Knight Rush
When to choose this: You are feeling confident. Your opponent seems weak or slow.
Build a Stable immediately.
Make Knights straight away. Do not wait.
Send knights to attack their villagers.
Research Hauberk at your Castle. This makes your knights very tough.
When you have a Castle, also make Serjeants.
Add Rams from a Siege Workshop when pushing buildings.
Keep attacking. Stopping lets them recover.
⚔️ Path B — Serjeant + Donjon Rush
When to choose this: You feel safer playing defensively. Or your opponent is aggressive.
Build a Castle as soon as you have 650 stone. (You already have 100 — mine 550 more.)
Make Serjeants from your Castle.
Send Serjeants forward. They can build Donjons near the enemy base.
A Donjon near their gold or wood will terrify them. This is attacking — just slower and safer.
Add Rams once Serjeants are established forward.
Add Knights later when you have gold to spare.
You are not turtling. You are creeping forward. That is good.
What are they building? Here is what you do.
Find what they have. Read what to build. It is that simple.
They build
🏹 Archers
Build Knights.
Knights are fast. They close the gap before archers can run away. Archers cannot win against knights in a straight fight.
Your knights take less bonus damage than normal. They will survive more arrows than you expect.
They build
🏹 Crossbowmen
Build Knights. Add Serjeants too.
Same as archers. Knights close fast. Serjeants have high armour and tank the bolts. Put Serjeants in front, knights behind.
Your soldiers take 40% less bonus damage. Crossbow bolts deal bonus damage — your units survive them better than others.
They build
🗡️ Spearmen or Pikemen
Build Serjeants. Add Rams.
Spears are designed to counter knights. Do not send knights into a spear blob. Serjeants are infantry — spears are less effective against them. Rams soak arrow fire and protect your Serjeants.
Your Serjeants take less bonus damage too. They are tougher than normal infantry.
They build
🏇 Scouts or Knights
Build Serjeants. Add Spearmen too.
Serjeants are tough and tank cavalry hits well. Spearmen are cheap and specifically counter cavalry. Use both together. Spears in front, Serjeants behind.
Remember — your soldiers take less bonus damage. Your Serjeants will survive cavalry hits better than you think.
They build
⛪ Monks
Build Knights. Keep them moving.
Monks convert units that stand still. Keep your knights moving at all times. A moving knight is very hard to convert. Send Light Cavalry to kill the monks directly — they are fast enough to catch them.
They build
💥 Mangonels or Siege
Rush with Knights immediately.
Two knights kill a mangonel very quickly. Do not let siege fire at your army. Close the distance fast and attack the siege directly. Mangonels cannot defend themselves up close.
They build
🏰 Towers or Donjons
Build Serjeants. Add Rams.
Rams absorb tower fire so your Serjeants stay alive. Never attack a tower without Rams in front. Move Rams up to the tower, let them soak the arrows, then let Serjeants finish it.
Your Serjeants take less bonus damage from tower arrows. They are more durable near towers than most units.
🛡️ If you are not sure what to do — do this
Build a Donjon near whichever resource feels most at risk.

Your walls and towers build 50% faster than other civs. A Donjon goes up quickly and will immediately start helping.

Then take a breath. Look at what your opponent is building. Find it in the table above. Build the answer.

You are not losing just because they are attacking. Sicilians are one of the hardest civs to kill. Keep building. Keep producing. You are tougher than you think.
👑 First Crusade — Save this for Imperial Age
First Crusade is your most powerful technology. It spawns 5 free Serjeants at each of your Town Centers.

If you have 3 Town Centers, that is 15 free Serjeants instantly.

When to use it: When you are ready for a big final push. Or when your army has been destroyed and you need soldiers immediately.

Do not forget you have this. It can turn a bad situation into a winning one very quickly.
