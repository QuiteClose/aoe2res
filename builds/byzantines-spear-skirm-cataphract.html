Counter awareness
🏹 ArchersElite Skirms — −25%, specifically counter archers. Mass them.
🏹 CrossbowsSkirms + Cataphracts. Cataphracts close fast. Logistica shreds grouped crossbows.
🗡️ Spears/PikesCataphracts. Spears deal reduced bonus vs Cataphracts. Logistica returns +6 vs infantry.
🏇 Scouts/KnightsSpearmen — −25%, direct counter to cavalry. Cheap and fast to make.
🐪 CamelsCamel Riders — yours cost −25%. Or switch to infantry + skirms entirely.
⛪ MonksKeep Cataphracts moving. They're more resistant to conversion. Light Cav to snipe monks.
💥 MangonelsRush with Cataphracts. Two kill a mangonel instantly. Never let siege fire freely.
🏰 Towers/CastlesRams + escort. Never attack fortifications without rams absorbing fire.
