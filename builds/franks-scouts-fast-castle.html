Counter awareness — how to respond
Spears / PikesThrowing Axemen from castle shred them. Or split knights to avoid spear blobs.
CamelsFranks have no camel answer. Throwing Axemen + siege or transition to infantry.
CrossbowsKnights close the gap fast. Approach from multiple angles. Don't charge a massed formation.
MonksKeep knights moving — stationary knights get converted. Light cav to snipe monks.
MangonelsSpread knights — never bunch. Rush mangonels directly, they die to 2 knights.
ScorpionsSame as mangonels — rush them. Scorpions are helpless vs knights at close range.
